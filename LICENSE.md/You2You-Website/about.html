If you don't know already, my name is Dan Goresht, but don't ask me how to pronounce my last name because I honestly can't tell you.

I have a degree in computer science, I once was diamond rank in League of Legends, I have played on the Magic the Gathering Pro Tour and I will never decline a game of Super Smash Bros.

Magic the Gathering is my main hobby, and I have been playing competitively since Return to Ravnica. It didn’t take me long before I starting playing UW control decks, and now I have this website where I write all my opinions about the architype. If you haven’t noticed I named this website “You2You”, that’s a sorta play on words to represent “UW” which stands for “Blue White” in magic lingo.

Don’t get me wrong, I love UW, but you might catch me on other decks at time to time. I play pretty much exclusively in paper, and try to play 2-4 times a week. I wouldn’t ever consider myself a pro, but I am a local grinder and I try to put out consistent results at every event.

I enjoy standard and legacy, but modern is my favorite format.
